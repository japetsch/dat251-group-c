# SAF Features / requirements

## Technical
- T1, immediate: Interoperability between different hospitals / blood banks
	- Feature: change "home" blood bank
	- One single, shared system
- T2, immediate: ease of registration / operating the app
- T3, immediate: ease of scheduling appointments
	- Integration with personal calendar app?
	- Notifications delivered by app
- T/E4, immediate: notifications regarding usage of donated blood ("you saved a life today!")
	- Economic cost, social gain?
- T5, immediate: notifications regarding demand surges for specific blood types
	- Especially universal donor
- T6, immediate: accessibility / universal design. In this including a way of registering without the app / website
- T7, immediate: high availablility / stable system
- T8, immediate: blood donations in review (yearly) / statistics
	- related to gamification of the system

## Social
- S1, immediate: (following from T1) Seamless process when moving
- S2, enabling: (following S1, T3, T/E4, E4, T8) stable, increasing donor count
	- Recruitment, and preventing loss
- S4, enabling (following E2, E3, T/E4, T8): promoting donor well-being
- S5, systemic (following E2, S4): word-of-mouth advertising the concept
- S6, systemic (following S7): good quality health care, extending peoples lifespan
- S7, enabling (following T5, S2): enough blood available

## Environmental
- EN1, immediate (following T1): energy savings

## Economic
Cost/benefits:
- E1 (following T6): People answering calls
	- Ideally we should minimize this
	- Quick replies needed to ensure T2/T3
- E2, ??: Gifts
- E3, ??: Soda, candy, etc.
- E4, systemic (follow S5): advertising the concept of blood donation
	- Could also directly financially purchase this
	- Ref: "bleed for your football team" campaign
- E5, immediate (following T1): need for fewer IT system administrators
