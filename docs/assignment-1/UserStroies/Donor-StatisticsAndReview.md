# Statistics and periodical review

*As* a blood donor,
I care about my contributions to society,
so *I want* to see a record of my donations, 
data about their impacts,
and be reminded of this periodically,
*so that* I remain motivated to continue donating.

## Acceptance criteria

- User can see a record of their donations
- User can see either:
  - Estimates about the utility of their donations, or
  - Actual data about how their donation was used
- User can receive a periodic (yearly/bianually/quarterly etc.)
  report about their donations
